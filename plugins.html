Plugins

What are plugins?

Plugins are pieces of software that can be used inside a DAW to add, enhance, edit or otherwise process audio in a variety of ways. This ranges from creating a series of chrods with a guitar plugin, to taking out the low frequencies on an instrument with an equalizer plugin.


To the left, a plugin called Kontakt is featured. It allows you to buy different virtual instruments and sounds and use them to make music.
